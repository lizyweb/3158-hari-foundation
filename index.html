About Us

Hari Foundation

Hari Foundation is committed to improving lives through accessible healthcare, education, and empowerment. We believe in holistic development, promoting moral, intellectual, and cultural growth. Focused on research excellence, we aim to enhance livelihoods and create health awareness. Supporting traditional medicine and preventive care, our mission is to ensure quality education and accessible healthcare for all citizens, fostering a healthier, informed society.

Introduction

Hari Foundation Trust is an NGO that aims at the upliftment of women and children from various circumstances that limit their access to basic needs, and collaborates with organizations for various educational initiatives. It aims to help individuals fulfill some of these needs and empowers them to learn, dream and live a better life.

Hari foundation has started in the year 2010 and properly registered under the trust act in the year 2013 by Dr.D.Manivannan . He is a academician and research guide in the nursing discipline of different universities. It is started with the aim at reaching out every citizen to improved quality of life, accessible health care, empowerment and enhancing livelihoods.

We believe, education is the total development of individual in moral, intellectual, physical, emotional, social, cultural and spiritual. We continue our efforts to enhance the quality of research which is the need of hour for the development of country. The foundation is also focus on the prevention of illness to create awareness regarding the health and illness to the general public. We promote quality and accessible health care and also traditional system of medicine.

Pillars of Our Organization

Trust

We exhibit trust in our interactions with our supports, donors, well-wishers, funding agencies, benificiaries, partners, suppliers, employees and one another.

Servitude

We create a work environment where serving others always takes preceedence. We belief that true success is measure by how we serve others

Discipleship

We develop our team members to have value and pride in all that they do. We help them grow as good stewards in their family and community. It is a standard for us to extend into the future.

Integrity

Our charecters and, ultimately, our success are based upon our integrity, fairness and honesty towards our collabrators.

Vision

We envision creating a better India in achieving accessible health care, education and empowerment to every citizen and aims at enhancing the quality of life, health, research, rural development and culture preservation and livelihood promotion.

Mission

> To provide quality and accessible health care to all the individuals.
> To enhance the health work force through establishment of health care institution, capacity building measures and setting standards.
> Advancing research especially in the field of public health and nursing.
> To empower every individual by means of socio economic development, skill development and vocational training and education.
