Activities

Free Medical Camp

Diabetic camp was organized in the month of December 2023 at Sri Hari Foundation Rajapalayam. A total of 100 patients with diabetes were participated in the camp. Blood Glucose and Blood Pressure monitoring was done. Education regarding causes, types, signs and symptoms, complications and measures to control diabetes was explained. Yoga education on diabetes management was given.
Free Asthma camp was conducted at Hari Foundation Skill Training centre, Rajapalayam along with Lions Club of Rajapalayam in November and December 2022.

Tiger Conservation Training for Teachers

Training was given to the school teachers on Tiger conservation at Auditorium, Lions Club of Rajapalayam trust. The programme was organized along with Zoo outreach trust. A total of 30 teachers were participated in the event. The training was given for 3 days in November 2022.

Essay and Drawing Competition

Essay and Drawing Competitions were organized every year in the eve of Independence Day at Government Higher Secondary School, Chinniyampalayam, Coimbatore District. Prizes were distributed to the participants.

Awareness about Artisan Identity Card

Awareness was given to the traditional embroidery trained artisans about the Identity card giving by Handicraft department and also gave education about the benefits of the card in the Hari Foundation Skill Training centre at Rajapalayam in March 2023.

Environment Protection – Tree Plantation

Hari Foundation planted 50 trees with the help of Self-Help Group Women around Rajapalayam, aiming to improve livelihoods, promote environmental awareness, and empower communities through education, health care, and sustainable development efforts for a better future.

Career Counseling Programme

Hari Foundation organized a Free Guidance and Counselling Programme for plus-two students in February 2022, attracting participants from various schools. The initiative aims to empower students with essential insights for their academic and career paths, fostering education as a means to enhance individual and societal development.

International Women”s Day

Hari Foundation celebrates International Women’s Day annually on March 8 at Lions Building, Rajapalayam, with engaging competitions, cultural events, and delightful refreshments, fostering empowerment, community spirit, and inclusivity.

Health Education on Menstural Hygiene for Adolescent Girls

Health Education Programme on Menstrual Hygiene was carried out by Mrs. S.D.Subha to Adolescent school girls at Government High School, Murambu, AKDR Women College and Skill Training Centre, Lions Building, Rajapalayam.

Nutrition Education Programme

Nutrition Education Programme was conducted at Lions Building to Women of Self Help Group at Lions Building, Rajapalayam. Nutitional Educational Programme was carried out in 5 sessions. The Nutrition Education on Prevention of Nutritional disorders and National Nutirional Programmes was given. A total of 15 women participated in the programme in each session.

Breast Cancer Awareness Programme

Video teaching programme on Breast self-examination is conducted every year for the women of Self-Help Groups. 40 women are participating every year in this program. The Programme was organized in Lions Building, Rajapalayam.

Yoga activities

Hari Foundation promotes yoga activities to enhance physical, mental, and spiritual well-being. By integrating traditional practices, we aim to improve health, prevent illness, and empower individuals for a balanced, healthier lifestyle. Join us in embracing yoga for accessible healthcare and holistic development in every aspect of life.
